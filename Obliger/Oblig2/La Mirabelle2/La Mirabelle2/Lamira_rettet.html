La Mirabelle

About us

La Mirabelle is a family run combined restaurant, bar and café.

We opened our doors for the first time in 1999, and have been serving quality dishes and good drinks ever since. La Mirabelle is centrally located at Barcode, Bjørvika, where we have been since the beginning. Our kitchen is open from early in the morning until well into the evening, and we have something to offer everyone! We are proud to be a place many return to time and time again.

Welcome to us, and experience our cozy and informal atmosphere where you will feel at home. We look forward to your visit!
